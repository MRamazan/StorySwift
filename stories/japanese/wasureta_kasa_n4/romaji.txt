Akiko wa nijuugosai no kaishain desu. Kyou wa shigoto ga owatte, densha de ie ni kaerimashita.
Eki ni tsuita toki, ame ga tsuyoku futte imashita. Akiko wa kasa wo dasou to shimashita ga, kasa ga arimasen deshita.
"A, densha ni wasurete shimaimashita!"
Akiko wa isoide eki no wasuremono sentaa ni ikimashita. Madoguchi de ekiin ni hanashikakemashita.
"Sumimasen. Densha ni kasa wo wasurete shimattan desu ga."
"Itsu no densha desu ka." to ekiin ga kikimashita.
"Sanjuppun mae no densha desu. Aoi kasa deshita."
Ekiin wa oku no heya wo sagashi ni ikimashita. Sukoshi matta ato, ekiin ga modotte kimashita.
"Kore desu ka."
"Hai! Sore desu. Arigatou gozaimasu. Hontou ni tasukarimashita."
Akiko wa fukaku orei wo iimashita. Ekiin wa egao de iimashita.
"Ki wo tsukete kudasai ne."
"Hai, korekara motto ki wo tsukemasu."
Akiko wa kasa wo motte, anshin shite ie ni kaerimashita. Soto wa mada ame ga futte imashita ga, Akiko wa kasa ga atte yokatta to omoimashita.
