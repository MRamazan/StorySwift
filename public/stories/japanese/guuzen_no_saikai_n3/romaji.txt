Hiroshi wa sanjussai no ginkouin desu. Aru doyoubi no gogo, itsumo no kafe de hon wo yonde imashita.
"Hiroshi? Hisashiburi!"
Furimuku to, mioboe no aru kao ga arimashita.
"Masato! Hontou ni hisashiburi da ne. Juunen buri kurai?"
Futari wa kodomo no koro kara no shin'yuu deshita. Koukou sotsugyou go, chigau michi wo erande shimai, renraku ga todaete imashita.
"Suwatte yo. Ima, nani shiteru no?" to Hiroshi ga kikimashita.
"Jitsu wa, resutoran de shefu wo shite irun da."
"E, ryourinin ni natta no? Tashika, oya wa hantai shite ita yo ne."
"Un. Oya wa daigaku ni itte hoshikattan da kedo, ryouri no michi ni susumu koto ni shitan da. Saisho wa taihen datta kedo, ima wa jibun no mise wo motsu no ga yume nan da."
Masato no me wa kagayaite imashita. Hiroshi wa sukoshi fukuzatsu na kimochi ni narimashita.
"Sugoi na. Ore wa antei wo erande ginkou ni shuushoku shita kedo, saikin, kore de yokatta no kana tte kangaeru koto ga arun da."
"Hiroshi rashii sentaku da to omou yo. Demo, shiawase tte hito sorezore da yo ne."
"Sou da na. Masato wa suki na koto wo shigoto ni shite, hontou ni shiawase sou da."
Futari wa yuugata made hanashikomimashita. Wakaregiwa, Masato ga iimashita.
"Kondo, ore no mise ni kite yo. Ryouri wo tsukutte ageru kara."
"Zettai iku yo. Mata renraku suru ne."
Ie ni kaeru michi de, Hiroshi wa kangaemashita. "Antei mo taisetsu da kedo, jounetsu wo motte ikiru koto mo daiji nan da na."
Kono saikai ga kikkake de, Hiroshi wa jibun no jinsei wo mitsumenaosu koto ni shimashita.
