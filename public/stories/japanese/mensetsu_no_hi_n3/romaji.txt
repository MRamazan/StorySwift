Naomi wa kotoshi daigaku wo sotsugyou shita bakari no nijuunisai desu. IT kigyou no mensetsu wo ukeru koto ni nari, mainichi junbi wo shite imashita.
"Kono kaisha ni hairetara, enjinia toshite hatarakeru noni."
Mensetsu no zenjitsu, Naomi wa kagami no mae de nando mo renshuu shimashita. Demo, kinchou shite nemuremasen deshita.
Mensetsu toujitsu, Naomi wa suutsu wo kite kaisha ni mukaimashita. Uketsuke de namae wo tsutaeru to, kaigishitsu ni annai saremashita.
Futari no mensetsukan ga matte imashita.
"Honjitsu wa oisogashii naka, ojikan wo itadaki arigatou gozaimasu."
Mensetsukan no Tanaka-san ga shitsumon shimashita. "Naze heisha wo shibou saremashita ka."
"Onsha no atarashii gijutsu e no torikumi ni kyoumi wo mochimashita. Tokuni..."
Naomi wa kotaete iru tochuu de, junbi shite ita naiyou wo wasurete shimaimashita. Aserimashita ga, shinkokyuu wo shite shoujiki ni iimashita.
"Moushiwake gozaimasen. Kinchou shite wasurete shimaimashita. Shoujiki ni moushimasu to, onsha de hataraite iru senpai kara, shain wo taisetsu ni suru shafuu da to kikimashita. Watashi mo sono you na kankyou de seichou shitai to omotte orimasu."
Mou hitori no mensetsukan, Yamamoto-san ga egao de iimashita. "Sunao de ii desu ne. Kinchou suru no wa touzen desu."
Sono go, Naomi wa ochitsuite hoka no shitsumon ni kotaeru koto ga dekimashita.
Isshuukan go, Naomi ni denwa ga kakatte kimashita.
"Saiyou ga kimari mashita node, gorenraku itashimashita."
Naomi wa shinjiraemasen deshita. Ureshikute namida ga desou deshita.
"Arigatou gozaimasu! Ganbarimasu!"
Denwa wo kitta ato, Naomi wa omoimashita. "Kanpeki denakute mo, seijitsu de areba tsutawaru n da."
Kono keiken kara, Naomi wa jibun rashiku iru koto no taisetsu sa wo manabimashita.
